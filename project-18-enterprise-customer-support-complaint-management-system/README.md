# 🚀 Project 18 – Enterprise Customer Support & Complaint Management System

## Overview

This project demonstrates an Enterprise Customer Support & Complaint Management System developed using Java.

The system manages customers, complaints, support agents, complaint resolution, escalation handling, service quality monitoring, and reporting.

It simulates enterprise customer support platforms such as:

- Zendesk
- Freshdesk
- Salesforce Service Cloud
- ServiceNow Customer Service Management
- HubSpot Service Hub

---

# Business Scenario

A company receives customer inquiries and complaints daily.

The customer service team requires a centralized platform to:

- Register customers
- Log complaints
- Assign support agents
- Track complaint status
- Escalate unresolved cases
- Record resolutions
- Generate service reports
