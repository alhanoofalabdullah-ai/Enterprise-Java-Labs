# 🚀 Project 11 – Enterprise Asset Management System

## Overview

This project demonstrates an Enterprise Asset Management System developed using Java.

The system manages company assets, equipment assignments, maintenance records, asset status, and inventory reporting.

It simulates enterprise asset management platforms used by IT, facilities, operations, and infrastructure departments.

---

# Business Scenario

An organization owns multiple physical and IT assets such as laptops, routers, servers, monitors, and office equipment.

The asset management team needs a centralized platform to:

- Register assets
- Track asset status
- Assign assets to employees
- Record maintenance activities
- Monitor asset availability
- Generate asset reports

---

# Objectives

- Asset Registration
- Asset Assignment
- Maintenance Tracking
- Asset Status Monitoring
- Inventory Reporting
- Enterprise Asset Governance

---

# Core Modules

## Asset Management

- Register assets
- Track serial numbers
- Monitor asset category
- Track asset status

## Assignment Management

- Assign assets to employees
- Track active assignments
- Release assets

## Maintenance Management

- Record maintenance actions
- Track maintenance history
- Monitor assets under maintenance

## Reporting

- Total assets
- Assigned assets
- Available assets
- Maintenance records
