# 🚀 Project 02 – Enterprise Customer Relationship Management (CRM) System

## Overview

This project demonstrates an Enterprise Customer Relationship Management (CRM) System developed using Java.

The platform helps organizations manage customers, sales opportunities, leads, interactions, and business relationships through a centralized environment.

The system simulates enterprise CRM platforms such as Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics 365, HubSpot CRM, Oracle CRM, and Zoho CRM.

---

# Business Scenario

A company manages hundreds of customers and sales opportunities.

The sales department requires a centralized platform to:

- Manage customer profiles
- Track sales leads
- Monitor opportunities
- Record customer interactions
- Generate sales reports
- Improve customer engagement
